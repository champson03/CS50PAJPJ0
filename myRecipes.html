Ginger Pork

    For two people

  • Pork Loin・・・400g
  • Ginger・・・60g + 30g
  • Garlic・・・1 clove
  • Onion・・・1/2 + 1/4
  • Salada oil・・・50cc
  • Olive oil・・・50cc
  • Butter・・・preferred amount
  • Light Soy sauce・・・preferred amount
  • Dark Soy sauce・・・preferred amount
  • Salt & pepper・・・preferred amount
  • Chikin cube・・・1 cube
  • Potato starch or corn starch・・・preferred amount
  • broccoli・・・preferred amount
  1. Making ginger oil

    2. Grate the ginger, garlic and onions. Mix it with dark soy sauce and olive oil (or salad oil).

  2. Marinate the pork

    3. Marinate the pork loin in the ginger oil for 10 to 20 minutes. (reserve the ginger oil)

  3. Cook rice

    4. Put the rice in the pot and wash the rice with water several times. Add water to about 1 cm above the rice and boil over medium-high heat. When it comes to a boil,stir. Simmer over low heat until water is moderately absorbed.

  4. Fry the meet

    5. Heat the salad oil in a frying pan and fry the pork. When the surface is fragrant, cook over preheated heat.

  5. Make the ginger sauce

    6. Saute the shredded ginger (30g) in butter. Add the onions (one quarter) and fold them. Add the Chikin cube. Add the ginger oil from the meat and light soy sauce and pepper. Add the potato starch or corn starch.

  6. Boild the vegetables

    7. Boild the vegetables for a few minitues.

  7. Arrange on the plate

    8. Dice the pork, put it on top of the garnish and pour ginger sauce over it.

Taco Rice

    For three people

  • Old el paso taco mix ・・・1 pack
  • Minced beef ・・・500g
  • Lettuce ・・・preferred amount
  • Tomato ・・・ 1
  • Cucumber・・・15 slices
  • Coriander・・・preferred amount
  • Salsa sause・・・preferred amount
  • Sour cream・・・preferred amount
  • Rice・・・1 and 1/2 Cups
  • Cheese・・・preferred amount
  1. Chop vegetables

    2. Chop the tomato into bite-sized pieces, shred the lettuce, slice the cucumber and chop the coriander to a fine.

  2. Cook rice

    3. Put the rice in the pot and wash the rice with water several times. Add water to about 1 cm above the rice and boil over medium-high heat. When it comes to a boil, stir it. Simmer over low heat until water is moderately absorbed.

  3. Fry the meat

    4. Fry the meat according to an instructions on the back side of the taco mix.

  4. Arrange the food on a plate

    5. Arrange the rice, cheese, vegetables and meat on a plate in that order, and top with salsa sauce and sour cream to your liking.
